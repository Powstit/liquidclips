## Transcription speed ceiling on Intel Mac (2026-05-21 benchmarks)

| Engine + model | 58s audio | Speedup |
|---|---|---|
| faster-whisper tiny (int8) | 22.6s | **2.6x real-time** — current |
| whisper.cpp tiny.en-q5_1 | 48.5s | 1.2x real-time |
| whisper.cpp base.en-q5_1 | 204.8s | 0.3x real-time |

Intel Mac CPU has no AVX2 (`whisper-cli` reports `SSE3 | SSSE3 | ACCELERATE | OPENMP` only). whisper.cpp's biggest gains come from AVX2/AVX-512 — gone on this hardware. **faster-whisper is the right local engine for Intel without AVX2**; engine swap doesn't help. Real "super fast" path is hosted Whisper for paid tiers — scope before building.
