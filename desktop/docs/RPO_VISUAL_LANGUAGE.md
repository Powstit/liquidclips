# Liquid Clips — Ready Player One Visual Language

> **Status**: in flight (2026-06-03). Locked anchors + character; asset generation pending credit top-up.

## The pitch in one line

The Liquid Clips UI should feel like a **cinematic gaming experience** inside a creator's sanctuary in the OASIS — Ready Player One staging, Blade Runner 2049 atmosphere, brand fuchsia as the signal colour.

## Locked references

These two assets are the **visual bible**. Every subsequent Higgsfield generation passes them as `medias` references so the world stays continuous.

### `assets-wip/anchor/oasis-anchor-LOCKED.png`

21:9 cinematic frame. Vast cosmic interior chamber suspended in deep velvet-black space. Translucent holographic glass panels float at varying depths, edges glowing with hot magenta-fuchsia neon, soft white core lights inside. Volumetric pink + pale cyan particles drift through suspension. Mirror-black ground recedes into void with a subtle hexagonal grid faintly catching the panel glow. Anamorphic lens flares across the upper third, soft optical haze. **No people, no logos, no text.** Generated by Nano Banana Pro at 2k.

### `assets-wip/character/hero-character-LOCKED.png`

3:4 portrait. **Kade** — 16-year-old white teenage boy. Messy chestnut-brown hair over forehead, earnest hazel eyes, faint half-smile. Charcoal hoodie under slate-blue technical vest with thin glowing fuchsia trim along the seams. Hexagonal grid floor at his feet. Magenta rim light upper-right, cyan key lower-left. **He is the avatar of the user inside the OASIS** — relatable underdog hero, not pretty-boy model. Generated by Nano Banana Pro at 2k against the OASIS anchor.

## Palette + lighting rules

| Token | Value | Used on |
|---|---|---|
| `--fuchsia` | `#FF1A8C` | Primary signal — glowing edges, CTAs, level-up moments |
| `--fuchsia-bright` | `#FF3DA5` | Hover states, particle bursts |
| `--fuchsia-deep` | `#C70066` | Pressed states, shadow tints |
| `--cyan-cool` | `#00E5FF` | Secondary signal — fills, status OK, ambient particles |
| `--paper` | `#F4F1EA` | Text on dark surfaces (cinematic film white, not pure white) |
| `--void` | `#0A0A0F` | Backgrounds — never pure black, always with depth haze |

**Lighting always**:
- Hot fuchsia rim from upper-right or upper-left
- Cooler cyan key from below-front
- Volumetric particle drift in the air column
- Anamorphic flare across the upper third when the camera faces a panel
- Hexagonal grid reflection on any horizontal surface

## Generation protocol

1. **Every gen passes both locked refs as `medias`** — anchor for environment, Kade for character.
2. **No prompt invents new visual elements** — only camera moves, motion, lighting variations within the established vocabulary.
3. **Save raw outputs to `assets-wip/<surface>/`** with versioned filenames (`v1.mp4`, `v2.mp4`).
4. **Promote to canonical** by copying to `desktop/src/assets/brand/` or `marketing/img/` only after Daniel approves.
5. **Topaz upscale** any 720p output we want to promote to 1080p before shipping (cheap, preserves credits up front).

## What this is NOT

- ❌ Anime style — Kade is photoreal, not stylised
- ❌ Cyberpunk neon overload — fuchsia is the only signal; everything else is restraint
- ❌ Corporate SaaS clean — density and atmosphere over whitespace
- ❌ Audio cues (not yet) — fake the *feeling* of sound with motion + glow
