Refunds

Refund policy

Try Liquid Clips with confidence. If it's not for you, we make it easy to get your money back.

Last updated: 24 May 2026

14-day money-back on subscriptions

If you're not happy with a paid Liquid Clips subscription, email us within 14 days of your first payment and we'll refund it in full. There's a generous Free tier so you can try the core workflow before paying at all.

Founder (one-time)

The Founder plan is a one-time purchase that unlocks Agency-level access for life. It's refundable within 30 days of purchase provided it hasn't been substantially used. After that window the Founder purchase is non-refundable, since it's a lifetime unlock.

Renewals

Subscriptions renew automatically. You can cancel anytime to stop future renewals; cancelling keeps your access until the end of the current paid period. Renewal payments after the initial 14-day window aren't automatically refundable, but reach out if something went wrong — we're reasonable.

How to request a refund

  • Email support@liquidclips.app from your account email with your order details.
  • If you purchased through Whop, refunds are processed per Whop's billing terms — we'll help you sort it.
  • Refunds return to your original payment method, usually within a few business days of approval.

Exceptions

We may decline refunds in cases of clear abuse (for example, repeated buy-refund cycles or fraud). Affiliate commissions and Whop Content Rewards payouts are separate and governed by their own terms.

Bring-your-own-key usage with third-party AI providers (e.g. OpenAI) is billed by that provider directly and is outside our refunds.
