Release notes for the desktop app and platform. We ship fast.

Reliability & privacy pass

  • Tightened analytics so no filenames or local content can ever be sent.
  • Referral links now lock attribution reliably from any entry point.

Built for clippers

  • Reworked the whole experience to lead with the Whop Content Rewards workflow.
  • Adopted Whop's own language ("Content Rewards") across the app and site.
0.4.12May 2026

Content Rewards workspace

  • Pick a reward and Liquid Clips keeps the payout, platforms, rules, source, and submission link in one place.
  • Per-clip fit checks against the brief: length, captions, platform, keywords.
  • Resume in-progress reward projects from where you left off.

Earn, wired up

  • Browse live Content Rewards inside Liquid Clips, routed securely through the backend.
  • Account debug panel so you always know which plan and identity are active.

The foundation

  • Results workspace with editable titles, captions, and per-clip layouts.
  • First-run onboarding, settings, OS-keychain key storage, hardware checks.
  • Publish-now, scheduling, and 14-day auto-drip for paid tiers.
