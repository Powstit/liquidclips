03

Launch and sign in.

First boot installs the local Python sidecar — give it a few seconds. Sign in with your account email to unlock your plan.

First-launch warning?

If Gatekeeper says the app can't be opened, right-click Liquid Clips in Applications and choose Open, then confirm. The build is signed with a Developer ID certificate and notarized by Apple — the prompt only appears on the very first launch from the DMG.

What runs locally

  • Faster-whisper transcription runs on your machine. Audio never leaves your device for local-tier workflows.
  • ffmpeg cuts & reframes on your machine. Exports land in your ~/LiquidClips folder.
  • Hosted AI is opt-in on Pro and Agency tiers — those features clearly say "hosted" when enabled.
  • Auto-update is built in. Future releases install in place from inside the app. You only need to download the DMG once.
