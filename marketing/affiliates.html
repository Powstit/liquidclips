How commission works

Simple, honest, recurring.

Questions

Affiliate FAQ.

Who can join?

Anyone with an audience of clippers or creators. Sign in with Whop to get your tracked link. Commission payouts require an active paid Liquid Clips subscription (Solo or up).

When do I get paid?

Commission becomes payable once you reach Qualified Partner status, then recurs each month your referred customers stay paid. The qualification details are in the affiliate terms. Whop handles the payouts.

What happens if I cancel my Liquid Clips plan?

Future commissions pause while your subscription is inactive. Reactivate and eligible commissions resume from that point forward. Commissions already paid to you are yours to keep.

What's the starter pass my audience gets?

Approved invite links can unlock 100 reward clip exports before Solo billing starts. New clippers add a card and bring their own OpenAI key. (Rolling out — ask in the partner dashboard.)

Does Liquid Clips submit clips to Whop for them?

No. Liquid Clips prepares submission-ready clips and helps track the submission link — clippers post and submit on Whop themselves. Whop tracks views and pays out.
