# Liquid Clips landing page upgrade

> Created 2026-05-28.
> Purpose: reframe the landing page around reward clippers, not generic AI editing.

## New page job

The landing page must answer one street-level question fast:

> Can this app help me make more money clipping reward campaigns without wasting time or missing rules?

Do not lead with "AI video editor." Lead with the money workflow.

## New hero

### Headline

The clipping command center for reward campaigns.

### Subcopy

Browse paid campaigns, keep the brief beside your editor, generate batches of clips, and track what gets posted, approved, and paid.

### Primary CTA

Download Liquid Clips

### Secondary CTA

See the clipping workflow

## Above-the-fold visual

Show the actual product:

- Left: Liquid Clips Earn workspace with campaign cards.
- Right: in-app Whop/browser panel open to a reward page.
- A small active brief strip: "$2/1k views · TikTok + IG · captions required · product visible."

This visual should prove the product in one glance: campaign on one side, workbench on the other.

## Copy blocks

### Workflow section

**Find the campaign. Keep the rules. Make the clips.**

Most clipping money is lost before editing starts: bad campaigns, missed rules, expired budgets, wrong platforms. Liquid Clips keeps the campaign brief attached to the work so every clip has a job.

### In-app browser section

**Whop on the right. Liquid Clips on the left.**

Browse rewards, open briefs, check payout terms, and keep your clipping workspace open. No tab juggling. No guessing which campaign the clip belongs to.

### Batch section

**Volume without the burnout.**

Import the source video, generate candidates, review the strongest hooks, and export a campaign batch into one organized folder.

### Submission section

**Track the money loop.**

Add the post URL, mark what was submitted, and track views, approvals, rejections, and estimated payout by campaign.

## What to remove or demote

| Current focus | Decision | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| "Runs your whole channel" | Replace | Too broad; reward clippers need campaign workflow. |
| Generic AI editor copy | Demote | OpusClip already owns that language. |
| Auto-publish/drip | Keep beta/lower | Useful later, but not the PMF wedge. |
| Hosted AI | Keep private beta/lower | Not the launch promise. |
| Affiliate program | Move lower | Users first need to earn by clipping, then by referring. |

## Brand voice rules

Use:

- "Find better campaigns."
- "Keep the brief beside the clips."
- "Make more eligible clips."
- "Submit cleaner."
- "Track what gets paid."
- "Double down on winners."

Avoid:

- "Unleash creativity."
- "Revolutionary AI."
- "Passive income."
- "Guaranteed payout."
- "Autopilot your channel."

## Landing page success check

The page works if a new clipper understands these five things in under 10 seconds:

1. Liquid Clips is for reward campaign clipping.
2. It has an in-app campaign browser.
3. It keeps campaign rules attached to editing.
4. It helps produce batches, not one clip at a time.
5. It tracks submissions and payout routes.
