Liquid Clips is becoming the desktop command center for reward clippers.

Payout route map

Reward campaignUser clips for Whop/Clipify/external campaign.
→
Platform submissionUser posts manually, then submits link/upload on campaign platform.
→
Paid by campaign platformWhop rewards are paid by Whop. External campaigns are paid externally.

Liquid Clips referralUser refers paid Liquid Clips customers.
→
Stripe ConnectAffiliate completes Express onboarding and KYC.
